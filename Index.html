Happy Birthday

Mi Reina! I hope you remember me. Today holds a special place in my heart— it’s the day the world was graced with your presence. The first time I saw you, sitting on the back of scooty with your father, I fell for you in an instant. On this special day, I can’t help but think of you, wishing you all the love and happiness in the world. And your eyes were like windows to another world, a place where golden sunsets melted into the horizon and the warmth of honeyed love lingered in the air. They held the mystery of dark, ancient woods, pulling me in with the promise of secrets untold. In their depths, I saw burning stars, shimmering with the mysteries of the universe, vast and unexplored. They were as profound and captivating as the deepest ocean, making it impossible to look away. And in that moment, I fell, completely and irreversibly, into their endless allure.

If I were a poet, I would describe your eyes like this:
"Eyes like golden skies, as if they have drowned the sun within them.
Eyes that remind me of the sweetness of honey, the amber warmth of love.
Eyes like dark woods, where one could be lost and never found.
Eyes like burning stars, mysteries of the universe yet to be discovered.
Eyes as profound and captivating as the depths of the ocean.
Eyes worth loving and dying for.
Eyes like a starry night carrying the secrets of the universe. They say mysteries often make you lose your mind. I have already lost mine."

Then, on our tour of Purulia, I had the chance to get to know you better, and I found myself falling even deeper for you. I never mentioned it, but it was in Purulia that everything began. During our trip to Puri, that evening when we sat together on the beach was a precious moment for me, one that I will always cherish. But I know you don’t care about that. That’s when I finally confessed my feelings. Later, when you weren’t feeling well on the bus, I was really scared. But the days after returning from Puri were some of the best of my life. I miss talking to you, your little moments of anger, and fulfilling your wishes. Your bracelet still remains with me as a lucky charm, and I still keep your photo. I saw you a few days ago but didn’t have the courage to speak. However, due to some misunderstandings, everything ended. I had thought of a wonderful gift for your birthday, but it all fell apart before I could give it to you. I really, really miss you, your family, and your sister dearly. I will never forget you. If you ever want to talk to me, know that I will always wait for you. I hope life treats you kindly and you find happiness wherever you are, even if I’m not by your side.

I hope someday you will talk to me ,
someday you will call me ,
someday you will again call my name ,
and someday you will also fall for me .
